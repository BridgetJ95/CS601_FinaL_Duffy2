Knitting

I started working on fiber crafts in undergrad as a way to manage anxiety. I find it really relaxing to have something to do with my hands while I play D&D or listen to podcasts, and it's so satisfying to be able to see my progress through a project I'm making with my own hands.

While I started with crochet, I now almost exclusively knit.
