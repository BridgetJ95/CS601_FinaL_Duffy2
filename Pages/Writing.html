Writing

Beginning

My love of books and active imaignation led me to writing at an early age. I loved creative writing assignments at school, and I ran the creative writing club while in high school.

Education

I decided to focus my education on Creative Writing. I majored in Fiction Writing in college and got a certiifcate in Children's Literature as well, as young-adult fantasy always held a special place in my heart.

Rather than pursuing a job first, I decided to go right into grad school. I got my MFA in Writing for Children at Simmons University. It was a fantastic experience; many academic circles turn their noses up at children's literature, and it was a joy to spend two years of my life focused on writing with a group of people who loved and respected children's media as much as I do. While I was there, I completed two full manuscripts.

Now, I'm focusing on my day job; for the time being, I write fiction about my and my friends' D&D campaign in my spare time.

Award and Publication

I had a short story published in vol. 6 of the literary magazine Wizards in Space. Titled "Full of Grace," it deals with the complicated feelings that come from being gay and raised Catholic.

In 2020, I won the Susan P. Bloom Discovery Award for one of the manuscripts I wrote in grad school.

My short story and more information about the award can be found on my personal website.
