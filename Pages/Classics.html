Classics

"What is Classics?"

I actually got this question a lot as a Classics major! Many assume "Classics" means Classical works of literature, but the field of Classics is actually the study of Ancient Greece and Rome.

Why Classics?

I was really interested in Greek Mythology as a child. I enjoyed the stories, and I loved fantasy to begin with. Loving the mythology felt like an extension of that love.

I've never had a desire to make a career out of classics; I got a major in Classical Civilization and a minor in Latin purely for fun. After I graduated, my parents took to Rome! It was great to be able to tour the city and see the sites from the history I'd spent four years studying.
